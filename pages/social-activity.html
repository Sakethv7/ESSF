Social Activity

Our contributions to society — from disaster relief to eco-conservation

Highlights

Delhi University Endowment Fund
Happy and proud to announce that our Director Sh. Jagdish Sehgal donated ₹1,00,000 from his own funds to the Endowment Fund of Delhi University.

🏺

Say No to Plastics — Clay Bottle Drive
Distributed free clay bottles among members and the general public with the slogan "Say no to plastics and save the Environment."

🏔️

Himachal & Uttarakhand CM Relief Fund
ESSF donated ₹1,50,000 towards the Himachal Pradesh CM Relief Fund and ₹1,00,000 towards the Uttarakhand CM Relief Fund as part of our social responsibility.

🫁

Covid-19 Relief — Oxygen Concentrator
Made an oxygen concentrator available during the COVID-19 emergency period for critical patients in need.

😷

Free Mask Distribution
Distributed free masks to the community during the Covid-19 pandemic to protect lives and prevent spread.
