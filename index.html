What We Do

Incorporated 18 September 2020

Dedicated to preserving India's rich cultural heritage, nurturing young and talented artists, and inspiring communities through meaningful initiatives — including the promotion of sports, the fight against plastic pollution, scholarships for deserving young minds, and the restoration of Maa Yamuna.

Activities Undertaken

📅

Yearly Wall Calendars

Annual calendars on the themes of Save Environment, Wildlife, and Heritage of India — spreading awareness beautifully.

🎓

Scholarships

₹12,000 per student, every year — scholarships for meritorious students from financially weaker sections of society.

💃

Annual Grand Event

A flagship event promoting Indian classical dance heritage — performance opportunities for young artists paired with a Save Environment & Education for All symposium.

💧

Save Water & Clean Yamuna

Campaigns spreading awareness about water conservation, plastic-free living, and cleaning Maa Yamuna.

👜

Jute Bag Distribution

Free distribution of jute bags to actively discourage single-use plastic in daily life.

🏺

Eco Water Bottles

Free distribution of clay / terracotta water bottles and water thermos to replace plastic bottles.
